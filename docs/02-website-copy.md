# Tradely — Website Copy & Section Structure

Australian English throughout. Tone: confident, warm, plain-spoken. Every primary CTA carries anxiety-reducing microcopy.

---

## Homepage

**Headline:** Get the right tradie for the job — fast.
**Subheadline:** Post your job for free and compare quotes from licensed, reviewed local tradespeople across Australia and New Zealand.

**Primary CTA:** `Post a Job — it's free`
**Secondary CTA:** `Browse jobs` (for tradies)
**Hero microcopy:** Free to post · No obligation to hire · Quotes usually within hours

**Sections:**
1. Hero with inline starter form (service + postcode → launches wizard)
2. Trust strip — "12,000+ jobs completed · 4.8★ average rating · Licence-checked trades"
3. Popular categories chip grid (Plumbing, Electrical, Painting, Carpentry, Landscaping, Roofing, Tiling, Handyman…)
4. How it works — 3 steps:
   - **Tell us what you need** — Describe your job and add photos. Takes about two minutes.
   - **Compare quotes** — Licensed local tradies send you quotes with prices and timelines.
   - **Hire with confidence** — Check reviews and credentials, then hire the right person for the job.
5. Featured trades cards (photo, trade, suburb, rating, review count, Verified badge)
6. Testimonials (homeowner + tradie mix, with suburb and job type)
7. Tradie cross-sell band — **Headline:** "Tradies, turn local jobs into your next customers." **CTA:** `Join as a tradesperson`
8. Final CTA band — **Headline:** "Ready to tick it off the list?" **CTA:** `Post a Job — it's free`

## Post a Job page (wizard)

**Headline:** What do you need done?
**Subheadline:** Answer a few quick questions and we'll match you with the right local trades.

**Steps (one question per screen on mobile):**
1. **Category** — searchable grid of services. Microcopy: "Not sure? Pick the closest — tradies will confirm the details."
2. **Details** — title + description with prompt chips ("What's the problem? · When did it start? · Any access issues?")
3. **Photos** — drag-and-drop / camera upload. Microcopy: "Jobs with photos get 2× more quotes."
4. **Location & timing** — suburb autocomplete; timing chips (Urgent / This week / Flexible).
5. **Budget (optional)** — range slider or "I'd like quotes first".
6. **Contact** — email/phone → creates account or signs in. Microcopy: "Your contact details stay private until you accept a quote."

**Persistent elements:** progress bar, `Back` link, `Save draft` (auto-saves), trust footer: 🔒 "Your details are never shared without your permission."

**Confirmation screen:** "Your job is live! Local tradies are being notified now — most jobs get their first quote within a few hours." CTA: `Go to my dashboard`.

## Browse Jobs page (vendor lead feed)

**Headline:** Your next job is waiting.
**Subheadline:** Fresh leads from homeowners in your area, matched to your trade.

**Layout:** filter bar (trade, distance radius, timing, budget, sort by newest) → lead cards (title, category icon, suburb + distance, posted time, photo count, budget signal, "3 quotes so far") → gated detail for non-members ("Join to see full details and quote").

**CTAs:** `View & quote` (member) · `Join to quote` (guest).
**Empty state:** "No leads match your filters yet — widen your radius or add a service category. We'll email you the moment something comes in."

## Tradesperson Signup page

**Headline:** Grow your business with quality local leads.
**Subheadline:** Join thousands of AU & NZ tradies winning work on Tradely. You only quote on the jobs you want.

**Sections:** value props (Quality leads near you · Pay only to quote, or go unlimited · Build a reputation that wins work) → 3-step signup (Business details & ABN/NZBN → Licence & insurance upload → Services & service area) → earnings/social proof ("Tradies on our Pro plan win an average of 6 jobs a month") → tradie testimonials → FAQ accordion (lead pricing, verification time, cancellation).

**CTA:** `Create my free profile`
**Microcopy:** Free to join · Verification usually within 1 business day · Cancel anytime

## Client Dashboard

**Headline (empty):** Welcome back, {firstName}. What's next on the list?
**Sections:** active jobs (cards with quote count + unread messages), quotes needing review ("3 new quotes on *Leaking ensuite tap*"), messages preview, completed jobs awaiting review ("How did Dave's Plumbing go? Leave a review"), prominent `Post another job` button.
**Empty state:** illustration + "No jobs yet — post your first job and get free quotes from local trades." CTA: `Post a Job`.

## Vendor Dashboard

**Headline:** G'day {firstName} — here's what's happening.

## FAQ page

**Headline:** Frequently asked questions.
Tabbed by audience: **For Homeowners** (Is it free? How fast are quotes? Are trades licence-checked? What if something goes wrong?) · **For Tradespeople** (How much do leads cost? How does verification work? When do I see contact details? How do reviews work?). Each answer ends with a relevant CTA. Schema.org FAQ markup for SEO.
